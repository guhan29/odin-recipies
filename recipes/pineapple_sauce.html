Pineapple Sauce

Pineapple sauce for teriyaki chicken and Asian noodles.

Recipe Cooking Info:

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 10 mins
  • Total: 20 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • 4 slices canned pineapple in juice, drained and chopped
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons garlic powder
  • ¼ cup water

Steps:

  1. Combine the pineapple juice, teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, lemon juice, pineapple, sugar, and garlic powder in a saucepan over medium heat; cook until the liquid has reduced to a syrup, about 5 minutes. Stir the water through the mixture; continue cooking until the mixture thickens again. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
